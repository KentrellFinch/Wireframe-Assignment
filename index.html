Welcome to Mythara!

What is “Mythara”? Mythara is a 2-D Roblox fighting game that has a wide variety of characters you can choose from to fight your friends or you can fight other players in the competitive game mode to climb the ranks and to test your hand with others! Fun fact: This game was inspired heavily by Mortal Kombat.
